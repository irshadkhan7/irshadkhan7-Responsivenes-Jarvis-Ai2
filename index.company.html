Our story

“There’s A Way To Do it Better - Find

- Thomas A Edison
Sumit, Founder & CEO, JARVIS Invest, has imbued this quote in every aspect of his life. It
continues to be the driving force to this day, as JARVIS edges closer to completing one more year
in its journey from 2016.
Working across some of the leading financial institutions across the country, Sumit was
able to identify the problems that plagued the investor as well as the advisor. He was
inspired to create innovative solutions that can cater to the entire spectrum of equity
investors. An avid Avengers fan, his passion for technology combined with his belief that
Artificial Intelligence can bring about an unprecedented change in the way investment
decisions are taken led to the creation of JARVIS.
